I hope to be able to experience such relationships. Apart from all these skills and abilities, I would like to emphasize my love for helping people. I enjoy speaking out for others and guiding towards the best path possible, which in this case is God's path. Living not only a spiritual life but a life of righteousness has helped me significantly and I hope to help others in the same way.
